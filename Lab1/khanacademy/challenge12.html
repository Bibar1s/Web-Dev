Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Unsalted butter 1 cup (softened)
Granulated sugar 3/4 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Eggs 2 large
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3: In a large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.

Step 4: Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Step 5: Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.

Step 6: Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.

Step 7: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
